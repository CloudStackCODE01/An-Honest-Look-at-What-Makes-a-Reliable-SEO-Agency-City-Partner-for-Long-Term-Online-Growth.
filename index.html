What to Expect from Working with a Local SEO Agency City Team Like Ours

If you’ve never worked with an SEO team before, you might be wondering what to expect. Here’s a breakdown of how we typically work with city-based clients at Affordable SEO of San Diego:

1. Local SEO Audit

Every project begins with a thorough audit. We assess your website’s technical structure, current rankings, backlink profile, content quality, and local signals like your Google Business Profile. This gives us a baseline so we can identify what’s working and where we can improve.

2. Competitor and Keyword Research

Understanding your competition is just as important as understanding your own business. We take a deep dive into the competitive landscape of your city to see what your top competitors are doing—and how we can outperform them.

We also perform detailed keyword research focused on local search terms. This includes city-specific variations that potential customers are using in real-time searches. These aren’t just broad terms like “plumber” or “hair salon.” We look at terms like “emergency plumber San Diego” or “best hair salon near Balboa Park.”

3. Content Development

Strong content helps your website rank and builds trust with visitors. Our content creation approach focuses on helping your audience find the answers they’re looking for. Whether it’s a blog post addressing common questions or service pages that clearly explain what you do, we focus on making sure every word serves a purpose.

4. On-Page SEO and Technical Fixes

From title tags to internal linking, we take care of the behind-the-scenes elements that affect how Google sees your site. We ensure your metadata is clear, your site loads quickly, and your structure is easy for both users and search engines to understand.

5. Link Building and Local Citations

Quality backlinks and citations help prove to Google that your business is a legitimate and trusted source. We focus on building real connections with local directories, news outlets, and industry-relevant websites. No spammy link networks, no shortcuts—just clean, white-hat strategies.

6. Monthly Reporting and Strategy Adjustments

We believe in full transparency. Every month, we provide a clear and easy-to-understand report showing how your site is performing, what we’ve done, and where we’re going next. We adjust strategies as needed based on the data—not gut feelings.

Common SEO Mistakes We Help City Businesses Avoid

Many businesses come to us after trying SEO on their own or hiring someone who made big promises and delivered little value. Here are a few mistakes we frequently correct:
Targeting the wrong keywords: Without research, it’s easy to focus on keywords that don’t bring qualified traffic.

  • Over-optimization: Trying to “game” the system with keyword stuffing or duplicate content can lead to penalties.
  • Ignoring mobile usability: A city-based business needs a website that looks and works great on mobile.
  • Neglecting local listings: Your Google Business Profile, Yelp page, and other directories all need consistent, accurate information.
